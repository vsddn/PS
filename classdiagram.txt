The diagram is about Orders
The diagram shows 2 kinds of Orders:
	1. Special
	2. Normal
There is one more class named Customer that has attributes name and location and methods named sendOrder and receiveOrder which are used to make a request of order and reception of the order.

Order class has functions to confirm the order and closing of the order.

SpecialOrder and NormalOrder classes have functions that are used to show the confirmation of the order, distpatching the order, accept the order and close the ordering process.
